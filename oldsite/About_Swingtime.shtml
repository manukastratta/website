About Swingtime

Swingtime is a high energy swing dance troupe dedicated to bringing the fun, excitement, and flair of swing dancing to Stanford. Swingtime's performances are known not only for their fast-paced lindy hop with jaw-dropping aerials but also their smooth and sexy swing moves. You never know what to expect with Swingtime - they've even been caught swinging to hip-hop on occasion! You can find Swingtime performing at many dance events such as Ragtime Ball, Viennese Ball, Big Dance, and of course, the annual Swingtime Spring Show. Swingtime guarantees to bring a smile to every face at every performance!

Check out our pictures and video clips, and then see us in person at one of our performances or contact us to hire us for your next event or to make a general inquiry.
