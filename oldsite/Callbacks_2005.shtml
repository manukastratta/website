Swingtime 2005 Auditions: Callbacks

Swingtime is pleased to announce the Callbacks round of its 2005 auditions! Callbacks is the second round of our auditions process, in which a subset of our initial auditionees group is called back for a closer look. See our FAQ for more information about the group, auditions, etc.

When:Monday, October 10, 8:30-11pm
Where:Roble Dance Main Studio
What:Further work on partnering and lindy technique, review of last week's choreography, learning more choreography, and performances with peer reviews. The mp3 of the performance song, Non-Stop Flight, is available here.

Evaluation: Auditionees are evaluated based on a variety of factors including technique (both swing and more general dance techniques), style, lead/follow ability, and performance potential (ability to bring energy, fun, and poise to their dancing). Most of important of all factors are expressive dancing and personality, energetic performing, and ability to learn dance quickly. We observe auditionees (both live and on film) and consider feedback they receive from our members and from each other in our unique Peer Review process.

Callbacks Timeline:

  • Callbacks (Mon, Oct 10, 8:30-11pm): Callbacks are similar to auditions. However, with a smaller group, we are able to provide more individual attention, teaching, and feedback and perform more comprehensive evaluations.
  • Callbacks peer reviews (due Tues, Oct 11, 5pm): Same as auditions.
  • New members confirm joining group (by Thur, Oct 13, 5pm): New members are invited via email (sent on a rolling basis) to join and asked to confirm their membership within 24 hours. Once all slots are filled, the new group is annouced on our website and remaining auditionees are informed of our decision.
  • Retreat for all members (Sat, Oct 15, through Sun, Oct 16): Group retreat, mandatory for all new members.
