Swingtime is happy to announce its 2006 Callbacks. Auditionees who have been called back have been contacted by email and are asked to show up on the night of Wednesday, Oct 4, in Roble 52 (the old fencing room) from 7-9pm. Please show up at least 15 minutes (and preferably more) early.

Callbacks
Date:Wednesday, Oct 4, 7-9pm
Location:Roble Gym (Room 52)

Callbacks function as the first Swingtime rehearsal of the year, giving us an opportunity to see how auditionees fit into the group, as well as how quickly they can learn and master technique, choreography, and performance. They will be evaluated according to the same criteria used for auditions, but Swingtime members will have a chance to dance with auditionees more and take a closer look at each individual. Once again, auditionees again be asked to fill out peer review forms.

Callbacks auditionees are asked to review the choreography taught at auditions so that we can quickly move on to new material. To assist them, we're making some choreography notes available here as well as the music here. Good luck!

For questions, see our FAQ or email swingtime-info@lists.stanford.edu.
