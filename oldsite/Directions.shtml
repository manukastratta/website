Directions

Maps [ Stanford campus map | Mapquest ]

From I-280:

  1. Take the Page Mill Road exit east towards Palo Alto/Stanford
  2. Turn left onto Junipero Serra
  3. Follow Junipero Serra past Stanford Avenue and Campus Drive East
  4. Turn right onto Campus Drive West (third traffic light)
  5. Turn right onto Santa Teresa Street (first stop sign)
  6. After you pass several dorm buildings on your right and a grass field on your left, you will see Roble Gym on your left
  7. Park in the parking lot on your right
  8. After you enter Roble Gym, go straight through another set of doors then turn left to get to the Main Studio
From I-101:
  1. Exit at Embarcadero Road West/Stanford University.
  2. Follow Embarcadero Road through Palo Alto and cross El Camino Real to enter the Stanford campus.
  3. Stay in the left lane when the road splits in two.
  4. Turn right on Campus Drive (first stop sign)
  5. Follow Campus Drive around campus
  6. Turn left onto Santa Teresa Street (three-way stop sign)
  7. After you pass several dorm buildings on your right and a grass field on your left, you will see Roble Gym on your left
  8. Park in the parking lot on your right
  9. After you enter Roble Gym, go straight through another set of doors then turn left to get to the Main Studio
