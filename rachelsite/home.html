Hep to the Jive Since 2002

Swingtime is Stanford's premiere performance-based swing dance troupe that works with professional choreographers, dance historians, and dance legends to recreate swing-era dances and integrate current trends. Swingtime's repertoire ranges from smooth swing with sleek moves to fast-paced Lindy Hop with jaw-dropping aerials. We are continually pressing the limits of how smooth, fast, and high we can go.
